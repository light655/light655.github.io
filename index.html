A Taiwanese EE student doing weird things when he is supposed to sleep.

Hello! I am Liang-Yu Chen (陳亮宇) in real life and light655 on the internet. I am an undergraduate student in the Department of Eletrical Engineering in National Tsing Hua University, Taiwan. My academic interests include IC design and power electronics. My undergraduate research project is about galvanically isolated gate driver for GaN power transistors, which is advised by Professor King-Yuen Wong.

I also lead the 822 Maker Space in my department. I spend most of my free time over there developing all sorts of electronic modules and gadgets. We also started in robotics recently. Some of which made it to the finish line and are available for purchase on Tindie.

This website contains my technical blog and blab, documentation for some libraries I wrote, and the datasheet of my products. The blab page contains things that are worth while writing down but not long enough to make a blog post. For example, traps I found during development or interesting information I found on the internet.
